# Writing Style Profile

## Overview

Write 1 short paragraph describing the overall feel of the writer's prose.

## Core Tendencies

- List 5 to 10 recurring tendencies.
- Each point should be concrete and observable.
- Prefer statements like "often qualifies claims before stating the main
  judgement" over labels like "careful writer".

## Repeated Habits

- Note repeated constructions, framing phrases, transition words, or paragraph
  habits.
- Mention them only if they recur across samples.

## Strengths To Preserve

- List the stylistic traits that give the writing its identity or strength.

## Risks To Watch

- List the habits that create repetition, vagueness, stiffness, or drag.

## Operational Rules

- Optional section.
- Turn the profile into short instructions that can be reused in later editing.
- Example: "Prefer object-level claims over meta-writing about the paper."
